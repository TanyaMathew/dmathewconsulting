D Mathew Consulting is a new consultancy focused on strategy for the Financial Services market. The CEO and founder of this company, Dilip Mathew, spent his career building a reputation for his ability to manage large scale investment banking strategy and regulatory reform programmes. He gained international work experience over the span of his career, in London, New York, Zurich, Singapore and Sydney. From his entrepreneurial beginnings, he has come full circle to start his own consultancy, that brings together the wealth of experience he has gained with over 25 years of experience in the financial industry.
